“Thanks, it’s thrifted!”

What’s up! I’m Nikki—I live in New York City and I’m utterly obsessed with thrifting. With an (almost) 100 % thrifted closet, second‑hand shopping is not only a hobby of mine but also a sustainable lifestyle choice.

For the past 3 years, I’ve worked to create what I like to call The New York City Secondhand Guide—a curated list of thrift, vintage, and secondhand stores across NYC. It provides a deep examination into each store’s vibe, style, price range, location, price range, and more. Whether you're new to thrifting or a seasoned pro, The New York City Secondhand Guide makes the hunt easy and exciting with an interactive map that lets you input your personal preferences—helping you discover stores perfectly tailored to your style, budget, location, and more.

For years this holy grail list was sitting in my notes app. I loved sharing it with friends and family but wanted to give it to a larger audience. I created Nik’s Thrifts in January 2025 with the hopes of helping the NYC community navigate all the thrift stores and shop sustainably. From a young age, I’ve had a love for the environment, animals, and sustainability. I graduated from Lehigh University in 2024 with a major in Environmental Studies.

With the rise of fast fashion, my passion for sustainable fashion arose. I’m currently a graduate student at Columbia University pursuing my Master of Science in Sustainability Management. I’m grateful for the opportunity to combine my love for the environment with my entrepreneurial mindset and creativity to drive meaningful change.

Thrifting is an adventure every time—you never know what hidden gems you’ll uncover. It’s a fun and exciting way to express yourself while supporting a sustainable lifestyle . Every find tells a story, and every purchase keeps clothes out of landfills.

Be sure to follow Nik’s Thrifts on Instagram and TikTok to stay in the loop on exciting events like thrift crawls, clothing swaps, and more! Keep an eye out for awesome collaborations too—like with Remake, a global nonprofit advocating for a more sustainable and ethical fashion industry! Nik’s Thrifts is more than just store recommendations— it's about creating community while improving the world around us. I’m always happy to chat or answer any questions you have! niksthriftsnyc@gmail.com
