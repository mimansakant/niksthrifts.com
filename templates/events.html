5/04 - East Village Thrift Crawl
In collaboration with Remake, a global nonprofit dedicated to sustainable fashion advocacy, the group ventured to Thrift NYC, L Train, Buffalo Exchange, AuH20 Thriftique, and The Re-Shop. We ended the crawl at Book Bar Club, where participants were able to grab a drink and continue socializing. Not only did this event offer a fun exploration for people with different levels of thrifting experience, but it also provided a sense of community and opportunity for conversation around sustainable fashion!
